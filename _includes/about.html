About Me

I'm a Ruby on Rails developer who loves to code and teach. I'm fully Japanese. For half of my life, I've lived in America and for the other half, I've lived in Japan. I like to travel, play music, and learn new things. Hear my music here. I'm always happy to help people out if I can.

Here a few things I have/am working on that I am passionate about:

  • TECHRISE

    • I'm a co-founder / mentor at TECHRISE. We teach Nepalese youth how to code for two reasons: 1. I love teaching coding and want to spread my passion. 2. Being able to code would give them access to better job opportunities. Check us out here.

  • Tier1Golf

    • I'm a web developer at Tier1Golf. It's essentially a social network for golfers and allows them to connect, see each other's scores, and win prizes. Golfers can sign up for real tournaments on the website and go out and actually play it. After the tournament is finished, they are ranked and paid out according to their scores. Check it out here.
